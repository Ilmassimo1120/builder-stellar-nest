Australian Regulatory Compliance

AS/NZS 3000 Electrical Standards

What is AS/NZS 3000? The Australian/New Zealand Standard for safe electrical installation. It is mandatory compliance for all electrical work and covers AC installations, safety switching, earthing, and protection.

ChargeSource Implementation:

  • ‚úÖ Compliance checklist in Project Wizard (Step 5)
  • ‚úÖ Automatic items per charger/site type
  • ‚úÖ Documentation tracking (cert retention, inspection)
  • ‚ö†Ô∏è Audit trail incomplete (who signed off, when)

GST (Goods & Services Tax)

Australian GST is 10% and mandatory for businesses with turnover > $75,000. Quarterly BAS (Business Activity Statement) reporting is required.

ChargeSource Implementation:

  • ‚úÖ Auto-calculate GST in quotes (10% default)
  • ‚úÖ Global settings for GST rate configuration
  • ‚úÖ Quote totals: Subtotal ‚Üí GST ‚Üí Total
  • ‚ö†Ô∏è BAS export not implemented
